Indroduction to Computer Science

Computer Science is one of the disciplines of modern science under which, we study about the various aspects of computer technologies (hardware and software), their development, and their applications in the present world

It focuses on application of Computer technology in today’s life, information technology, computer threat, computer security, etc.

Principal areas of study within Computer Science include

  • Theory of computation
  • Algorithm
  • Artificial intelligence
  • Computer systems
  • Network security
  • Database systems

Some other domains are human computer interaction, vision and graphics, numerical analysis, bioinformatics.

Basics of Computer Science

Computer science is all about Problem Solving which involves :

  1. Giving some Input Data
  2. Processing the Input Data
  3. Producing the Output

“The computer was born to solve problems that did not exist before.”
